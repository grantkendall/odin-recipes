Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Roman dish of eggs, pork, and pasta.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 2 eggs plus 2 egg yolks, tempered
  • 1 ounce Pecorino Romano
  • 1 ounce Parmesan
  • 3 ounces guanciale, cut into lardons
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 12 ounces spaghetti

Method

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and both cheeses. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper.
  2. Fry guanciale in a hot skillet until crispy and remove from heat.
  3. Boil spaghetti in salted water until just before al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water and add pasta to pan with guanciale.
  4. Heat a bowl with hot water while pasta finishes cooking in the pork fat. Dump water from bowl and add pasta. Stir in cheese mixture, adding pasta water when needed.
  5. Serve with more grated cheese and freshly cracked black pepper.
