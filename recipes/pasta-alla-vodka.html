Pasta alla Vodka

Description

A smokey and creamy version of the classic Italian-American pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 4 ounces smoked bacon, lardons
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp red-pepper flakes
  • 3/4 cup vodka
  • 28-ounce can of crushed tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 pound rigatoni

Method

  1. Heat oil in a skillet and fry the bacon until crisp. Add shallot, garlic, and red-pepper flakes and cook until shallot is softened.
  2. Turn heat to medium-low and add the vodka. Let alcohol reduce by half.
  3. Add tomatoes and a 1/4 cup of water and simmer until sauce thickens, about 10 minutes.
  4. Cook rigatoni in salted water until al dente.
  5. Turn heat to low and stir in the cream. Add the cooked pasta and Parmesan and stir until well-combined. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
  6. Serve in warm bowls, topped with more Parmesan.
